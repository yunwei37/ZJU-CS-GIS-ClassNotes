 Previous scene  |  Next scene  SCENE VIII. A plain near Actium. 
 Enter OCTAVIUS CAESAR, and TAURUS, with his army, marching  OCTAVIUS CAESAR  Taurus! 
 TAURUS  My lord? 
 OCTAVIUS CAESAR  Strike not by land; keep whole: provoke not battle, 
 Till we have done at sea. Do not exceed 
 The prescript of this scroll: our fortune lies 
 Upon this jump. 

 Exeunt  Shakespeare homepage  |  Antony and Cleopatra  | Act 3, Scene 8
